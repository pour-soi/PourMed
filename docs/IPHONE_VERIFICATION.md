# iPhone verification checklist

- Open your own instance URL, such as `https://your-project.workers.dev`, in Safari and add it to the Home Screen.
- Launch the Home Screen app and confirm the saved token authenticates, status loads, and the visible runtime diagnostics complete.
- Confirm the service worker is registered and no stale update prompt remains when no waiting worker exists.
- Confirm notification support is available, permission is shown, and the push subscription becomes active after enabling notifications.
- Review Today, History, Statistics, and Settings navigation at iPhone width.
- Use only disposable local or test-instance medication entries during verification.
- Confirm the offline shell loads and writes fail visibly while offline.
- Send a test notification only to your own device and instance. iOS controls sound, vibration, Focus, and presentation.
